Instructions

There are two players in this game. You are one of them. Both of you are suspected of committing crimes. The judge decides to offer you two a bargain: Note: betray = to say that the other person committed a crime

If you betray the other person, you are set free and the other person receives a 3 year sentence. If the other person betrays you, then he/she is set free and you receive a 3 year sentence. If you both betray each other, then you both serve two year sentences. If you both choose to remain silent, then both of you will serve only 1 year.
